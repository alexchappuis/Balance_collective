Our Mission

About

Hi, I'm Isa.

I'm an NBHWC board-certified health and wellness coach and the founder of Balance Collective. I studied psychology at Northeastern University and grew up in a Puerto Rican household where food, family, and taking care of each other were central to everything. Those roots have shaped everything I believe about wellness.

I grew up in a home where wellness was simply part of life. At one point, I drifted from those roots — my energy dropped, my mind felt overwhelmed, and I found myself doing movement I didn't enjoy with a complicated relationship to my health. Going back to the basics changed everything.

I created Balance Collective because I believe real wellness isn't complicated. We've just been pulled toward extremes and quick fixes, and away from what's simple and built to last.

— Isa, Founder of Balance Collective

Community

Club Balance.

Real moments, real connection, real wellness. Club Balance is an in-person community in Houston where people come together, feel safe, and actually enjoy taking care of themselves — whether that means making a new friend, getting creative, moving in a way that feels good, or simply being in a room with other people.

Every Club Balance event is designed around a different pillar of health — movement, mindfulness, creativity, nutrition, sleep, and community — because wellness is so much more than just working out and eating well.
